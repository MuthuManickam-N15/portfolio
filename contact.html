Frequently Asked Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple landing page typically takes 1-2 weeks, while a full website with custom functionality can take 4-8 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients from all around the world. I'm comfortable with remote collaboration and adjust my schedule to accommodate different time zones.

What technologies do you specialize in?

I specialize in modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (React, Vue, Node.js), and various backend solutions. I choose the best tech stack based on your project requirements.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! I offer various maintenance and support packages to keep your website running smoothly. This includes updates, bug fixes, security patches, and content updates.

What are your payment terms?

I typically require 50% upfront to begin work, with the remaining 50% due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

Can you help with website redesigns?

Definitely! I love breathing new life into existing websites. Whether it's a complete overhaul or just modernizing the design, I can help transform your online presence.
